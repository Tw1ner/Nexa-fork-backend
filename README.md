# Nexa Fork backend by @tw1nefr 
#  What new?
- New commands (User)
- /Balance (see your vbucks balance)
- /Changer-password (change your account password)
- /Changer-username (changer your account username every 2 days)
- /Claim your reward (Like Ultimate donator ect)
- /Daily Vbucks (Earn 150 daily vbucs)
- /Gift (gitf a in-game item to your friend)
- /Help (display help)
- /Leaderboard (see your in-game leaderboard)
- /locker (see your in-game locker)
- /Profile (display your profile like how many kills ect)
- /Register (Make a account)
- /Report (report in-game user they need a account)
- New commands (Admin)
- /Admin-stats (display the admin-stats panel)
- /ban (ban a in-game user or discord I think)
- /ban-status (see a in-game banned user)
- /Create-a-random-account (create a account for test or just for free play)
- /Give-full-locker (Give full locker to a person they need to have a account)
- /Give-items (give a person a in-game items with the item Skin ID)
- /Give-vbucks (give v-bucks to a person or yourself)
- /softban (Softban a discord user)
- /unban (unban a discord user)
